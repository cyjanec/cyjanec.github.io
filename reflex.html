EPICS CED - Reflex

FALL 2019 ~ SPRING 2020 - The Reflex project, is an interactive display designed to show the neural pathways involved in the knee-jerk reflex, and is now on display at the Children's Museum of Indianapolis.

As the team design lead, I collaborated with two other teammates to design this display. I'm responsible for the CAD design of power plant "chess pieces," which are the main component that children get to touch and interact with.

Design Considerations

  • Design for manufacturability
  • Part strength, safety, and longevity
